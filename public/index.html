Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data sent anywhere?

No. All spell checking happens locally in your browser. No network calls, no tracking, no data leaves your device.

How many words are in the dictionary?

The built-in dictionary contains 15,000+ common English words. You can add unlimited custom words.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Since everything runs locally, the extension works completely offline.

Can I use it on sensitive sites?

The extension automatically skips password fields and financial inputs. You can also disable it on specific sites or URL patterns.

Does it support other languages?

Currently supports English (US and UK). More languages coming soon!

How do I add words to my dictionary?

Right-click on a misspelled word and select "Add to Dictionary", or import a list of words in the Options page.
